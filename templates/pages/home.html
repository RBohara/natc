About US

NATC, Brisbane is a recreational sporting club which is not-for-profit, non-religious, non-racial, non-political, non-judgemental, non-discriminatory. The club respects and protects individual’s belief, race, ethnicity, academic background and skill level at all times. Main objectives of the club are to:
☞ Promote tennis in wider Nepalese community residing in Brisbane and all parts of Queensland
☞ Promote friendship, social harmony and collaboration among members, other associations and clubs by organising recreational and sporting activities
☞ Promote, motivate, inspire and provide platform for children within the community to pursue potential tennis career
☞ Tie up, collaborate, cooperate and build good relationship with professional tennis clubs and associations in Australia and Nepal to accomplish any activities as proposed and decided by the club
